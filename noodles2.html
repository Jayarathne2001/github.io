restaurant HOW TO MAKE CHICKEN NOODES

1. Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add sliced chicken and cook until browned and fully cooked. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

3. In the same pan, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil. Sauté sliced onion, minced garlic, julienned carrot, bell pepper, and broccoli florets until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

4. Return the cooked chicken to the pan with the sautéed vegetables.

5. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and black pepper. Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Toss everything together to coat evenly.

6. Add the cooked egg noodles to the pan. Toss and stir until the noodles are well-coated with the sauce and heated through.

7. Garnish the chicken noodles with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired.

8. Serve the Chicken Noodles hot and enjoy your delicious and quick meal!
