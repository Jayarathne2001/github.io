restaurant HOW TO MAKE FRIED RICE

Preperation

1. Make sure your cooked rice is cooled and not too sticky. If using freshly cooked rice, spread it out on a tray to cool for a while or refrigerate it for about 30 minutes.

Cooking

1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.

2. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and scramble them. Once cooked, remove the scrambled eggs from the skillet and set them aside.

3. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil to the skillet.

4. Add diced carrots and cook for 2-3 minutes until they begin to soften.

5. Add frozen peas and diced ham or cooked chicken (if using) to the skillet. Stir and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

6. Add the cooled cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps.

7. Pour soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sesame oil over the rice. Stir well to evenly distribute the sauces.

8. Add the scrambled eggs back to the skillet and mix them into the rice.

9. Season with ground black pepper and salt. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

10. Stir in chopped green onions and cook for an additional 2 minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.

Serve

1. Remove the skillet from heat.

2. Serve the fried rice hot, garnished with additional green onions if desired.

Enjoy your homemade fried rice!
