restaurant HOW TO MAKE CHICKEN SOUP

Prepare the Chicken

1. Rinse the whole chicken under cold water and pat it dry with paper towels.

Boil the Chicken

1. In a large soup pot, combine the chicken and 10 cups of water. Bring it to a boil over medium-high heat.

2. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a simmer. Skim off any foam that rises to the surface.

3. Add diced onion, sliced carrots, sliced celery, minced garlic, bay leaf, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper to the pot.

4. Simmer the soup uncovered for about 1 to 1.5 hours or until the chicken is fully cooked and the flavors are well-developed. Skim off any additional impurities that rise to the surface.

Remove and Shred the Chicken

1. Carefully remove the whole chicken from the pot and place it on a cutting board.

2. Once cool enough to handle, shred the chicken into bite-sized pieces, discarding the skin and bones.

Add Noodles or Rice (Optional)

1. If using noodles or rice, add them to the pot and cook according to the package instructions.

Finish and Serve

1. Return the shredded chicken to the pot.

2. Adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.

3. Remove the bay leaf.

4. Serve the chicken soup hot, garnished with fresh chopped parsley.

Enjoy your comforting and nourishing homemade Chicken Soup!
