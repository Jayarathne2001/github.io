restaurant HOW TO MAKE PRETZELS

1. In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and kosher salt. Sprinkle the yeast on top and let it sit for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes foamy.

2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and melted butter. Pour in the yeast mixture, stirring to combine. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface until it becomes smooth and elastic, about 5 minutes.

3. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

4. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and lightly grease it with vegetable oil.

5. In a large pot, bring 10 cups of water to a boil. Add baking soda and reduce the heat to a simmer.

6. Divide the dough into 8 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a rope, about 20 inches long. Shape the ropes into pretzels.

7. Carefully place each pretzel into the simmering water for about 30 seconds. Remove them with a slotted spoon and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

8. Sprinkle the pretzels with coarse salt and bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.

9. Allow the pretzels to cool on a wire rack for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your homemade pretzels! You can also customize them by adding cinnamon sugar or other toppings of your choice.
